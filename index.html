Biography:

Hong Xie is currently a research professor at College of Computer Science, Chongqing University, supported by one hundred talents program of Chongqing University. He was a postdoctoral research fellow at Department of Computing Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong working closely with Prof. John C.S. Lui, and a postdoctoral research fellow at School of Computing, Nationial University of Singapore working closely with Prof. Richard T.B. Ma . He completed his Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2015, proudly under the supervision of Prof. John C.S. Lui. He received his B.Eng. degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology at The University of Science and Technology of China in 2010. His personal interests include movie, hiking, pingpang, basketball, etc. He is a member of IEEE and a member of ACM.

Research interests:

I am interested in data driven decision making problems, where I enjoy applying tools from statistics, machine learning, optimization, game theory, approximation algorithms, etc., to design models and algorithms that utilizes data to optimize decisions. In particular, I focus on the following three aspects:
  • Online learning theory and algorithms: Markov decision process, bandit learning, stochastic optimization, reinforcement learning, etc.
  • Multi-agent decision making theory: game theory, cooperative game theory, stochastic game, etc.
  • Applications: web applications like recommendation systems, E-commerce systems and distributed systems like networking.
